वक्रतुण्ड महाकाय सूर्यकोटि समप्रभ । निर्विघ्नं कुरु मे देव सर्वकार्येषु सर्वदा ॥


Our most awaited time of the year is finally here. The arrival of Ganpati Bappa, a time we celebrate with all pomp and gaity. We are overwhelmed to bring forth a lot of finely handcrafted, beautiful idols of the lord this year too; the same way we have been delivering perfection since 30 years. However, in the woke of the current environmental crisis, our determined makers of Lord Ganesha's idols strive to lend a hand by using "Shadu Maati" only as all previous years instead of other materials put to use that may cause any kind of harm to environment.All of this while taking into account the excitement and happiness that your family should feel while you look at the beautiful idols of our beloved Ganesha.

||Ganpati Bappa Morya||

(इथे फक्त मातीपासून बनवलेल्या मूर्ती मिळतील.)
